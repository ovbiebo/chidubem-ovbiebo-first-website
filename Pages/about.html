Values

  1. Be Your Own Customer

    My entire business relies on the same platform and tools my customers pay me for. To that end, I build products and design experiences I would want for myself.

  2. Empower Individuals

    I believe in the power of the individual to create great things. This is what my product enables and celebrates.

  3. Design Is Not A Luxury

    Good design means coalescing hundreds of details into a package that is simple and beautiful. From the pieces I create, to the experience my customers have, I strive for excellence in design and iterate relentlessly in order to achieve that ideal.

  4. Good Work Takes Time

    I respect the creative process and pursue long term ideas without fear. I only release my work when I feel it meets my standards.

  5. Optimize Towards Ideals

    While metrics are critical for tracking and testing the performance of my business, they are merely a reflection of my ideas and execution in the market. My values and ideals are my decision making guide.
